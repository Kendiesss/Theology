Be kind to others

Kindness is one of the most important ways to show God's love. When you are kind to others, you are showing them that you love them and that you care about their well-being. Being kind to others is a great way to be living manifestations of God’s kindness to His creation.

Forgive others

Forgiveness is another important way to show God's love. When you forgive someone, you are showing them that you love them and that you are willing to let go of any anger or resentment you may have towards them. By being a forgiving individual, we also reflect God’s mercy and show them that God is indeed merciful.

Love unconditionally

Unconditional love is the highest form of love. When we love unconditionally, we are showing others that we love them no matter what. This is the greatest way to show God's love. Through this, we can showcase to others the true meaning of Love which is the way God loves.

Donate to charity

There are many charities that are working to make the world a better place. When you donate to charity, you are showing others that you care about their cause and that you are willing to help them make a difference. This displays our innate generosity which subsequently gives people a glimpse of how generous God is with His abundant blessings.

Be a friend

Friendship is one of the most important things in life. When we are friends with someone, we are showing them that we care about them and that we are there for them through thick and thin. Promoting and exercising friendship will also make people realize how Jesus can be a great friend to all of us and that His good influence will bring us closer to God.
